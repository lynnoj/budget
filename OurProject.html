OUR PROJECT

How our project started

Project Ideas

Our idea originated from a discussion of how we would survive the semester at BCIT. We talked about how we would balance our courses, social life, and finances. Then we had to do a project for our COMP 1930 class, so we decided to create something that could actually help us during out time with BCIT. At first we thought of making a to-do list for our project. It would be simple, yet effective. However, we realized that we simply used other means of tracking our tasks, so we decided to go with "Budget", our budget and expense tracking app.

Font Choice

The fonts that we used for this project were two different san-serif fonts called "Raleway" and "Quicksand".

Colour Choice

Since our project has a water bubble in the home page, we decided to use a similar colour scheme to compliment the water. Our theme uses different shades of blue, with a dark desaturated blue being the main colour.
